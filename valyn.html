Doodle Jump

This game is where players control a character named "doodle" guide it upwards a platforms without falling to it. The goal is to climb as high as possible without falling of the screen or getting hit the obstacles. Players tilt their device to move the character left or right, guiding them to jump onto platforms. The platforms are usually arranged vertically, and they vary in size and characteristics.
