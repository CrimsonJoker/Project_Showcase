Tetra Puzzle

TetraPuzzle is an innovative and captivating puzzle game inspired by the classic Tetris. Set against a mesmerizing backdrop featuring five different Aurora, Zamboanga del Sur's delicacies that gracefully move and change, TetraPuzzle offers an immersive visual experience that enhances gameplay. The soothing and dynamic delicacy background not only adds to the aesthetic appeal but also creates a serene atmosphere for players to enjoy.
